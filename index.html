Welcome to JD Counseling

I am glad you're here! This is the first step in your journey.

JD Counseling provides mental health therapy for people in need. My goal is to provide a compassionate and empathic atmosphere using evidence based therapy. Educating and helping you make connections on the thoughts, feelings, and behaviors in your life will bring about positive change and help you rediscover yourself.
